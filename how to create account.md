# 🔥 Azure Free Account (Free Tier) kya hota hai?

Microsoft Azure ka free account tumhein deta hai:

* 💰 $200 free credit (30 days)
* 🖥️ Free services (12 months)
* 🔄 Kuch services hamesha free

---

# ⚠️ Requirements (Start karne se pehle)

Ye cheezein ready rakho:

1. ✔ Email ID (Gmail ya Outlook)
2. ✔ Phone number (OTP ke liye)
3. ✔ Debit / Credit Card (Visa / Mastercard)
4. ✔ Internet connection

👉 Note: Card se paisa cut nahi hota (sirf verification hoti hai)

---

# 🚀 STEP-BY-STEP ACCOUNT CREATION

---

## 🔹 STEP 1: Azure Website open karo

👉 Browser mein jao:

```
https://azure.microsoft.com/free
```

👉 Click karo:
**"Start free"**

---

## 🔹 STEP 2: Sign In / Create Account

Agar account nahi hai:

👉 Click:

* "Create one"

Fill:

* Email
* Password

👉 Click: Next

---

## 🔹 STEP 3: Email Verification

* Email pe code aayega
* Code enter karo

---

## 🔹 STEP 4: Personal Details Fill karo

Fill carefully:

* Country: Pakistan
* Name
* Address
* City

👉 Ye billing ke liye hota hai

---

## 🔹 STEP 5: Phone Verification

* Phone number enter karo
* OTP aayega
* Verify karo

---

## 🔹 STEP 6: Credit/Debit Card Add karo

Fill:

* Card number
* Expiry date
* CVV

👉 Important:

* Paisa charge nahi hota
* Sirf verification hoti hai

---

## 🔹 STEP 7: Identity Verification

Kabhi kabhi:

* SMS ya card verification hota hai

---

## 🔹 STEP 8: Agreement Accept karo

✔ Terms accept karo
✔ Click → "Sign up"

---

## 🔹 STEP 9: Portal Open

👉 Ab tum redirect ho jaoge:

```
https://portal.azure.com
```

---

# 🎉 DONE — ACCOUNT READY

---

* Home

1. Search → Resource Groups

## 🔹 Step 2: Free Services Check karo

Search:
👉 "Free services"
